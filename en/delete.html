Delete Your Account

StampWorld · account & data deletion

Delete in the app (instant)

Open StampWorld and go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account, then confirm. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted right away. This action cannot be undone.

Request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email support@stampworld.app from the email address associated with your account and ask us to delete it. We will verify your request and delete your account and data.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile (name, email, country), all of your progress (completed tasks, points, badges, passport, journal, past trips), your friends and friend requests, the photos you have uploaded — including task photos kept for moderation — and your subscription record on our servers.

Note: an active paid subscription is billed by Google Play and is not cancelled by deleting your account. Manage it in Play Store → Subscriptions.
